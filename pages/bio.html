About Me!

I attended All Hallows Roman Catholic High School and I left with 13 GCSEs and attained 3 A Levels and 1 AS Level in German, Modern History, Government & Politics and Classical History respectively at Blessed Cardinal Newman College.

At 18 I went to the University of Chester and studied History, completing my dissertation on the American Excellence argument for the Nuclear Age. While at university I was a member of the University of Chester rowing team for 2 1/2 years and raced in Nottingham and London to name a few and also did rowing privately for a prestigious team on the Chester River Dee. Chester is where I met my husband Alex and we live together in Bramhall and have recently got married on 25th February 2023.
